About RouteEat

Revolutionizing travel dining, one journey at a time

Our Mission

To make every journey delicious by connecting travelers with quality food, delivered right to their seats.

We believe that hunger shouldn't be a part of your travel experience. RouteEat was born from the simple idea that travelers deserve access to fresh, hygienic, and delicious meals during their journeys.

Our Story

RouteEat started in 2023 when our founder, during a long bus journey, experienced the common traveler's dilemma - limited food options, uncertain quality, and inconvenient stops. This sparked the idea to create a platform that would solve this problem for millions of travelers across India.

What began as a simple concept has now grown into a comprehensive platform connecting travelers with quality restaurants along their routes. We've partnered with hundreds of restaurants, ensuring that every meal meets our high standards of quality, hygiene, and taste.
